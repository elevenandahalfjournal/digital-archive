Q: melting like blood\
between my legs\
A: Love dissipates!

Q: the strongest point for me was love\
A: I – NEVER – WOULD – HAVE – THOUGHT

Q: like flimsy spaghetti;\
rolled round a fork\
A: pocket tomatoes

Q: No sir, I\
do not work\
with real players\
A: bleeding heart limelight\
ask for bleeding vulva

fin. thoughts (?):\
He's-so-hateable-just\
with-a-fuckable-face

he-yes-i-said-he\
No-not-me-i-won't\

Not-for-me-duh!

end panel.
